Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a tour?

Simply fill out our booking form with your preferred destination and dates. We'll confirm availability and send you a customized itinerary within 24 hours.

What's the best time to visit Mati?

December to May is ideal with sunny weather. However, Mati is beautiful year-round. We operate rain or shine with proper safety precautions.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes! Groups of 10+ receive special rates. Contact our team directly for custom group packages and pricing.

What's your cancellation policy?

Full refund if cancelled 2 weeks before. 50% refund within 2 weeks. Emergency cancellations handled on a case-by-case basis.

Are tours suitable for families with kids?

We offer age-appropriate activities and family-friendly packages. Our guides are experienced with children.

Do I need special certifications for diving?

PADI certification is recommended but not required. We offer beginner courses and supervised dives for all levels.
